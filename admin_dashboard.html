Vehicle Care & Insurance Concepts

Core Modules Explained

1. Policy Management

Storing insurance carrier name, policy number, and expiry. In this system, customers add this to their vehicle profile to enable 'Cashless' booking.

2. Surveyor Approval

When a claim is filed, the insurance company sends a Surveyor to inspect the damage assessment provided by our technicians. They approve the 'Estimated Amount'.

3. Settlement Calculation

Final Bill = Insurance Share + Customer Deductible. The deductible is a fixed amount the customer pays regardless of the insurance coverage.

4. Cashless Claims

The customer only pays the deductible at the counter. The service center collects the rest directly from the insurance company after work completion.

Project Data Flow

  • 1

    Customer books a service and checks 'Apply for Insurance Claim'.

  • 2

    Technician inspects the vehicle and enters 'Damage Assessment' details.

  • 3

    Admin reviews the assessment, assigns a Surveyor, and enters Approved Amounts.

  • 4

    Invoice automatically splits the total cost between Insurance and Customer.

System Configuration & Shares

Technician Share (Commission)

This percentage of the **Labor Charge** will be automatically added to the technician's earnings for every completed job.

Booking Capacity

Maximum number of vehicles the center can accept per day. Once reached, booking will be blocked for that date.

How Shares Work?

When a job is completed:

  • Technician receives: Labor Charge × {{ tech_commission_rate }}%
  • Shop receives: Remaining Labor + Parts Margin + Wash Fee

💡 Pro Tip: Increasing the share rate keeps your technicians motivated and rewarding high performance.
